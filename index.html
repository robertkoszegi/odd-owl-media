Our Work

Odd Owl Media is a multi-faceted production company. Our services can eb utilized in a breadth of settings: corporate videos, short films, multi-camera events, concerts and music videos, or even live streaming events.

About Us

Odd Owl Media was established in 2021 by Max Kennedy.
Max got his start in audio recording a mixing, which led to an in-depth, real-world education in video production. He fell in love with being able to take ideas from paper to screen, and after buying his first camera, he was hooked.
As a graduate from Humber College's Broadcast Television / Videography program, Max went on to work for companies such as Deluxe Post Production, Insight (Big Brother Canada), Castlewood (Cash Cab), and Portage Creative.
Clients include MT Walker, Orson Wilds, Glow Day Spa, Carey & Toni Nieuwhof, The Goldies band, Connexus Church.
